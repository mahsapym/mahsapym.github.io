RAG Research Assistant

Retrieval-augmented generation system for querying atmospheric science research papers in natural language. Implements page-level chunking with BGE embeddings, ChromaDB vector store, and a Streamlit chat interface with source-cited answers.

Deep Learning CMAQ Emulator

1D/2D CNN and U-Net models to emulate the CMAQ air quality simulation model, replacing numerical solvers with a deep learning inference pipeline. Applied SHAP to identify NOx and solar radiation as top prediction drivers.

EV Adoption Scenario Modeling

Well-to-wheel analysis for NYC, LA, Chicago, and Houston using GREET-MOVES integration. Multi-source data pipelines across transportation and energy datasets. Projected GHG and air quality impacts across 2035 EV scenarios.

Diffusion-Based Contrail Segmentation

Conditional diffusion model (DDPM/DDIM) for pixel-level contrail segmentation in GOES-16 infrared sequences. Multi-branch U-Net with RRDB encoders, Hough priors, and Fourier fusion. PR-AUC 0.345, skeleton recall 0.755 on 20,500+ samples.
